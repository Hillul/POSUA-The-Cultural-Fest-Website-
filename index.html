About Posua

The spring brings along hope , happiness ,adrenaline and more. Indeed there is no better way to welcome the season of positivity than with the Bohag Bihu festivities. Be it the beat of the dhol, the spark in the youth, the charm of the traditional assamese apparels or the ethnic fragrance of life, the Bohagi Festivities indeed add more value to the year.

MEMORIES

and it has been 4 years since we started celebrating
culture at it's zenith. Below are a few moments
that we wish to share with you.

2014

2015

2016

This year

Catch Zubeen Garg LIVE. He is an Indian singer, music director, composer, songwriter and actor who contributed to Assamese, Bollywood and Bengali. An auspicious night, a breathtaking performance, awaits for the charming crowd of NITs.

Kristi

India, the land of diversity has time and again been referred to as the melting point of various cultures.
The campus, bequeathed with greenery, is not only a place of technical excellencebut also emanates
cultural abundance. In tune with Bihu celebrations we present KRISHTI,where we showcase the breathtaking traditional dances and folk music of the various tribes of the state as well as country.


Gunjan

The beat of the dhol has many unexplored dimensions associated with it. The amalgamation of the traditional beats and tunes add spark to the atmosphere. With a view to explore the unexplored genres and enjoy the immaculate performance of the traditional performers, we introduce GUNJAN -the invitational “HUSORI BIHU” event.



Bordoicila


On this illustrious occasion, let us come together to experience the charm and beauty of an enthralling musical evening. The soul of the lyrics along with the rhythm of the music would only help us add so much more to the glittering experience.



Sparsh

Reliving the childhood memories is indeed a refreshing experience and there could be nothing better than an art competition to relive the days gone by. We present before you, SPARSH, the art competition, where we welcome tiny tots and school-going children from all across the Barak Valley.



Manthan

On the night of 1st April 2017, watch the great artist, Zubeen Garg performing for you live on the stage of NITs. Experience the greatest power of energy along with him, dance to your heart and let the energetic vibes flow through you. See him play for you and feel yourself being carried away by his melodious voice. You would not be able to stick your feet to the ground.



Ankuran

Life indeed is a journey which involves many challenges and rejuvenation of the body, mind and soul always adds value to what we may define as a healthy lifestyle. The “BOHAGI BIHU”, one of the most important festivals of the Assamese culture and tradition, is one such opportunity to celebrate life. With this view, we introduce ANKURAN.



Rangdhali

The charm and magic of the DHOL touches the lives of the kids too. The little ones also turn up to the occasion wearing the traditional attire like the charming Muga Silk. The excitement and passion of the kids indeed finds expression as they dance to the tunes of the enthralling music.



Pratidhani

PRATIDHANI is the platform where we come together to celebrate the core essence of the BOHAGI BIHU with the famous HUSORI competition. The energy reflected by the passionate artists transpires into the lives of everybody, thus offering us yet another reason to celebrate.
